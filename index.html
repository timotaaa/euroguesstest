How to Play EuroGuess

Guess the EuroLeague player in 10 tries! After each guess, see how close you were.

  • Team: Current team.
  • Position (POS): e.g., G, F, C.
  • Height (HT): In cm.
  • Age: Player's age.
  • Country (CTRY): Their origin.
  • Jersey (#): Their number.

✅ = correct. ↑/↓ = higher/lower.

The yellow box means you are within 3 units of the correct number, with the green box meaning you guessed the stat correctly!
